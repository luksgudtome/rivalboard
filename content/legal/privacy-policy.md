# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

## 1. Overview

Rivalboard ("we", "our", "us") provides a tournament management platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Account details: name, email address, and password hash.
- Authentication data: session cookies and login metadata.
- Tournament content: tournament names, descriptions, participants, match updates, and related data you submit.
- Technical data: basic request metadata needed for security and operations.

## 3. How We Use Information

- Provide and maintain the service.
- Authenticate users and secure accounts.
- Store and display tournament data.
- Improve reliability, security, and product quality.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on your location, we process information based on contract performance, legitimate interests, consent, and legal obligations.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with:

- Service providers that host infrastructure or support operations.
- Legal authorities when required by law.
- Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain account and tournament data while your account is active and as needed for legitimate business or legal purposes.

## 7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Your Choices and Rights

You can update account profile details and password from the account settings page. You may request account deletion or data-related requests by contacting us.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable safeguards.

## 10. Children's Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

## 12. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at: `support@rivalboard.com`
